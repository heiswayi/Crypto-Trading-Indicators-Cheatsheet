# Crypto Trading Indicators Cheatsheet

## **Primary Indicators**
| **Indicator**       | **Purpose**                | **Best Timeframes**        | **Typical Settings**       | **Best For**                            |
|---------------------|----------------------------|----------------------------|----------------------------|------------------------------------------|
| **Moving Average (MA)** | Identifying trends & support/resistance | 15m, 1H, 4H, Daily         | 9, 21, 50, 200 periods     | Long-term trends and smooth price action |
| **Exponential Moving Average (EMA)** | Trend-following in volatile markets | 1m, 5m, 15m, 4H           | 10, 20, 50 periods         | Quick trend changes and crossovers       |
| **Bollinger Bands (BB)** | Volatility-based strategy | 1m, 5m, 15m, 1H, Daily     | 20-period, 2 deviations    | Breakouts and mean-reversion trades      |
| **Parabolic SAR**    | Spotting trend reversals   | 5m, 15m, 1H, Daily         | Start: 0.01, Max: 0.20     | Trailing stop-loss in trending markets   |

## **How to Use Indicators**

### **1. Trend Confirmation**
- **Indicators**: EMA, MA, MACD.
- **Setup**: Use EMA (10, 20) or MA (50, 200) to identify trends. Confirm with MACD crossovers.

### **2. Volatility & Breakouts**
- **Indicators**: Bollinger Bands, MAVOL.
- **Setup**: Watch for price touching/extending outside Bollinger Bands with increased MAVOL to confirm breakouts.

### **3. Overbought/Oversold Levels**
- **Indicators**: RSI, WR, StochRSI.
- **Setup**:
  - RSI > 70 or < 30 indicates overbought/oversold.
  - Combine with WR (< -80 oversold, > -20 overbought) for better accuracy.

### **4. Reversals and Entries/Exits**
- **Indicators**: KDJ, Parabolic SAR.
- **Setup**:
  - Look for KDJ crossovers and Parabolic SAR dots changing positions to indicate reversals.
  - Use with trend indicators for confirmation.

## **Indicator Combinations**
1. **Scalping**:
   - **Timeframes**: 1m, 5m.
   - **Indicators**: EMA (10, 20), StochRSI, Parabolic SAR.
2. **Day Trading**:
   - **Timeframes**: 15m, 1H.
   - **Indicators**: Bollinger Bands, RSI, MACD.
3. **Swing Trading**:
   - **Timeframes**: 4H, Daily.
   - **Indicators**: MA (50, 200), MACD, RSI.
4. **Breakout Trading**:
   - **Timeframes**: Any.
   - **Indicators**: Bollinger Bands, MAVOL, RSI.

---

## **Tips for Success**
- **Combine Indicators**: Use trend, momentum, and volatility indicators together for robust setups.
- **Backtest**: Validate indicator settings on historical data for your preferred cryptocurrency.
- **Market Conditions**: Adapt indicators based on trending or range-bound markets.
- **Risk Management**: Always use stop-loss and manage position sizing based on volatility.
